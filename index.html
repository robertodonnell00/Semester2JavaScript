Lady Troffea

About the band

Upcoming Waterford band, sure to rock your socks off.



Lady Troffea is a band hailing from Waterford, Ireland that formed in late 2022. All members being multi-instrumentalists who have their own musical endeavours, but share a passion for strange and whacky music it was only innevitable that they would combine their efforts and form a band.

That band being "Lady Troffea". Lady Troffea's sound is hard to describe, it takes the randomness of psychedelic 60's, the grit from grunge, smoothness from jazz; and comibines them with the melodies of pop.

Still only in the early days of the band, they are working on recording and realeasing their first upcoming EP. Seeing as all members are working/in college, progress is slow going but the wait will be worth it!

The name "Lady Troffea" comes from the 1500's when a Frau Troffea began dancing in the streets of Strasbourg. People in the town began to laugh and jeer at her, but she didn't stop dancing. After a while, others joined her and they too couldn't stop dancing. Over the course of a month 400 people joined her and in their ceaseless dancing, died of exhaustion. It is still widely debated whether or not this story is myth or fiction, some describe it as a case of mass hysteria. One thing is for sure, when you see Lady Troffea, you're sure to be moved.
